CONTEXTin what situation was it composed?Main thrust:

reform: Hellas was to be a kingless society, unified by religion, language and culture, the political situation is to be frozen, to stop stasis

The story of Helen is a charter-myth of the Ionian colonization. Powerful groups, a.o. in Athens, are behind his. It is a long-term policy to conquer the western part of Anatolia, esp. the fertile valleys of the Hermos and the Maiandros rivers where the Lydians resp. the Karians and Maionians live.

This policy is a source of war; 1 is opposed to 2.

Smyrna and Milete are key cities in this, as is the Lydian stronghold of Sardis ("Troy" to the Smyrneans).

1 wins, emigration is redirected to the west and the black sea.
